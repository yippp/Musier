Introduction

- What is Musier? -

It provides the amateurs with
the chances to compose pieces of melody.

Music is a part of the daily life. And every of us enjoys various melodies.
Some amateurs may have the dream being composers, but lack professional knowledge.
It is quite difficult to acquire enough information right away in a completely unfamiliar area.
If you are the one who wants to be a composer, but feeling puzzled with the complex commands,
Musier is the one can do you a favour!
Make the choices from the lists & Enter your musical inspirations.
Surprises are ready for you!

Guideline

- Tutorials are all set! -

The following contents are parts of the guidebook for green hands.

1. Name the title of your product.

A charming name is needed for your later melody generated. Innovative ideas are wholly welcome!

2. Choose Major & Meter from the lists.

Major & Meter are the soul and skeleton for a piece of melody. You can also define them as rules.

3. Enter your ideas as the prologue.

Please tell Musier your thoughts by entering them in numbered musical notations by keyboard, like 11556654433221.
Guess the name of the example!~

4. Extra info. on inputs.

Number 1-7: musical notes
W, S: rise & drop an octave
A, D: shorten & extend the beat
J, K: flat & sharp
Backspace: delete the last input

5. "Click Here to Go" & Save your work!

The settings are ready for melody generation! Just click the button and wait for a while to achieve your midi file and staff sheet!

6. Change possible defects.

You can choose to improve the notations by changing specific details of the product on the bottom-left of the page before your saving.
